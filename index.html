Crosswords

One of my favorite hobbies is solving crosswords, especially cryptic crosswords.
I love how they’re not just about knowing words, but about figuring out clever clues that
hide double meanings, wordplay, or even little riddles. It feels like a mix of logic, wordplay,
and creativity all in one. Cryptics can be frustrating at first, but when the answer clicks,
it’s such a satisfying moment.
